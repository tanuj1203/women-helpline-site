Mission-Women Safety:-

To strive towards enabling women to achieve equality and equal participation in all spheres of life by securing her due rights and devising strategies for solution of specific problems/situations arising out of discrimination and atrocities against women and giving her best solution so as to take a strong step towards Women Empowerment which can help reduce social violence and atrocities against Women and help them to fight for their rights.
